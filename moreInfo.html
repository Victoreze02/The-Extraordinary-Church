Meet our Man of God

  • Apostle Paul Ojo




  • Apostle Paul Ojoh is the Senior of THE EXTRAORDINARY CHURCH (TEC) and the Founder of EARLY MORNING DEW (University of Jos). He is a graduate of Business Management from the University of Jos. He also have a diploma on The Power of the Mind from the Morris Cerullo School of Ministry. After graduating and serving in various ministerial capacity, God spoke to our Man of God as part of the Liberation Mandate to Leave Nigeria (Gen 12vs1) to the Kingdom of Eswatini (formerly known as Swaziland). A Country with less than One point Five Million Population with an advert rate of poverty. In Obedience to divine Mandate, this gave birth to LIBERATION MINISTRIES INT'L, Swaziland ( An interdenominational Ministry) where we were able to reach those in the interior part through organizing Crusade, Revival meetings, empowerment programs etc. We also run a monthly program in the capital city of Mbabane as well as our Man of God preaching on the Radio.

  • Lady Queen Paul




  • Lady Queen Paul is a Co-Pastor with Our Man of God. A graduate of Mass Communication from the University of Jos. She is a Writer, an Author and a Communication Experts.
